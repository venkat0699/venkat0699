## 🧑‍💻 About Me

I'm **Venkatesh Nekkanti**, a Senior DevOps Engineer with **6 years of experience** designing, building, and scaling cloud-native infrastructure. I live at the intersection of reliability, automation, and developer experience — if it can be scripted, it will be.

- 🚀 **Cloud-first mindset** — architecting scalable, resilient systems on AWS every day
- 🔧 **IaC fanatic** — Terraform is my second language; YAML might be my first
- 🐳 **Container enthusiast** — Kubernetes clusters don't scare me, they excite me
- 📊 **Observability nerd** — if it's not monitored with Prometheus & Grafana, does it even exist?
- 🤖 **Automation evangelist** — CI/CD pipelines, GitOps with ArgoCD, and shell scripts that do the heavy lifting
